Classic Waffle

Ingredients

  • 2 teacups of flour
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoons of salt
  • 2 spoons of corn starch
  • 3 beaten eggs
  • 4 tablespoons of melted butter without salt
  • 1 and 3/4 teacups of milk
  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla essence

Preparation method

  1. Sift the wheat flour, sugar, starch, yeast and salt into a container. Reserve.
  2. In another container mix the beaten eggs with the milk, the melted butter and the vanilla essence.
  3. Pour over the flour mixture and quickly incorporate the ingredients.
  4. Heat the waffle maker. Place a shallow shell of dough and spread it until it covers the mold of the device, close the lid and let it bake until the dough is golden brown.
  5. Remove with silicone spatulas. Serve with honey, fruit or jam.

Additional information

You can serve these waffles on breakfast with jam.
